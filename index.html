About SME Marketplace

SME Marketplace is a platform designed to empower small and local businesses across Ghana. Whether you are a farmer, a fashion designer, or a food vendor, our platform makes it easy for you to showcase your products and reach customers online with secure Mobile Money (MoMo) payments.

We believe in supporting grassroots entrepreneurship and helping communities thrive through digital inclusion. Join us in transforming local commerce, one sale at a time.

Our Mission

To empower small and medium enterprises (SMEs) by providing them with an easy-to-use online platform to reach more customers, grow their businesses, and embrace digital commerce.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted online marketplace for local businesses, where innovation meets opportunity and every entrepreneur can thrive.
